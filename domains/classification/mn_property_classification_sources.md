## 4. County Assessor Manuals and Classification Guidelines

- Counties like **Hennepin**, **Ramsey**, and **St. Louis** often provide:
  - Local interpretations of MN rules
  - GIS integration of classification maps
- Great for understanding **applied classification practices**.

---

## 5. Minnesota Tax Court Decisions

- **Link**: [MN Tax Court Opinions](https://mn.gov/tax-court/)
- **Purpose**: Legal decisions that clarify or interpret property classification and valuation law.
